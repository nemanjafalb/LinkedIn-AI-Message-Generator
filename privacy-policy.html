Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 20, 2024

1. Introduction

LinkedIn AI Message Generator ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

Our extension collects and processes the following types of information:

  • LinkedIn Profile Data: When you use our extension on a LinkedIn profile page, we temporarily access publicly visible profile information (name, headline, company, location, about section) to generate personalized messages. This data is never stored on our servers.
  • Usage Statistics: We store locally on your device the number of messages generated per month to enforce free tier limits.
  • License Information: If you purchase a premium license, we store your license key locally on your device.
  • API Usage: Generated messages are processed through OpenAI's API. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for how they handle data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Generating personalized LinkedIn messages using AI
  • Managing free tier usage limits
  • Validating premium license keys
  • Improving the extension's functionality

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All usage data and license information is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API.
  • No Server Storage: We do not store LinkedIn profile data or generated messages on any external servers.
  • Temporary Processing: Profile data is only temporarily processed to generate messages and is immediately discarded.

5. Third-Party Services

Our extension uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: For AI-powered message generation. Data sent to OpenAI is subject to their privacy policy.
  • LemonSqueezy: For payment processing and license management. We do not store your payment information.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • To OpenAI for message generation (profile data only, temporarily)
  • To LemonSqueezy for payment processing
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored data
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the extension
  • Opt out of using the service at any time
  • Request information about data processing

8. Data Retention

We retain:

  • Usage statistics: Until you uninstall the extension or monthly reset
  • License information: Until you uninstall the extension
  • Profile data: Not retained; processed in real-time only

9. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
